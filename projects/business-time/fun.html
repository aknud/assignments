Paragliding

Point of the Mountain Paragliding

Want to just have fun on a tandem flight? Or maybe get serious and learn to fly?
With 13 years of teaching expeience we share our knowledge of all types of conditions, challenges, and equipment. With us you will have a safe and fun flying experience - whether you are coming for the exhilaration of a scenic tandem, or to learn the exciting sport of paragliding.
POTMP supplies its students with only the latest and safest equipment (important when technology and design are advancing so rapidly), we hold the highest rating for instruction in the sport. Don't be fooled by low prices or vague promises - you will get the best deal, the most fun, and in-depth instruction when you are flying with us.

Goblin State Park

Explore Utah

Journey to this strange and colorful valley, which is unlike any other in Utah. The landscape, covered with sandstone goblins and formations, is often compared to Mars. Explore the geology, mountain bike, and camp among the nooks and gnomes.
