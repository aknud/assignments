Random Blog Post

January 29, 2019

The only prerequisite is that it makes you happy. If it makes you happy then it's good. See. We take the corner of the brush and let it play back-and-forth. Making all those little fluffies that live in the clouds. Be brave. As trees get older they lose their chlorophyll. You gotta think like a tree. We artists are a different breed of people. We're a happy bunch. We tell people sometimes: we're like drug dealers, come into town and get everybody absolutely addicted to painting. It doesn't take much to get you addicted. Now let's put some happy little clouds in here. We don't want to set these clouds on fire. Here's another little happy bush If we're gonna walk though the woods, we need a little path. If you do too much it's going to lose its effectiveness. You can do anything here - the only pre-requisite is that it makes you happy. You can do anything here. So don't worry about it. We'll put a happy little bush here. Let's build an almighty mountain. Even the worst thing we can do here is good. Maybe, just to play a little, we'll put a little tree here. In nature, dead trees are just as normal as live trees. When things happen - enjoy them. They're little gifts. This present moment is perfect simply due to the fact you're experiencing it. You're the greatest thing that has ever been or ever will be. You're special. You're so very special. Life is too short to be alone, too precious. Share it with a friend.
